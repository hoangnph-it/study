5 Levels of Claude Co-work
You Must Master

From basic chatting to a fully automated AI employee that reads your files, connects your apps, and runs tasks while you sleep.

The 5 Levels

1
Import
Transfer your memory and context from ChatGPT or Gemini to Claude in 60 seconds.
Use Anthropic's memory import page → copy prompt → paste in ChatGPT → get memory export → paste back in Claude. Done.
ChatGPT → Claude 60 seconds Zero friction
2
Foundation
Build the brain of your system — goals, company context, glossary, and operating instructions.
Write Goals MD, Claude MD, and Company MD files once. Co-work reads them every session. Self-learning system that improves over time.
Goals MD Claude MD Company MD Self-learning
3
Workflows
Install pre-built plugins or create custom command-based workflows for any department.
One command triggers an entire multi-step process. Legal contract review in 2 min. Custom YouTube analytics plugin. Share plugins with your team.
/review-contract /brief Custom plugins Team sharing
4
Ecosystem
Connect all your apps — Gmail, Notion, Slack, Canva, and 8,000+ more via Zapier MCP.
~38 native connectors + Zapier MCP for everything else. One prompt chains multiple apps together.
Gmail Notion Slack Canva Zapier MCP 8,000+ apps
5
Automation
Schedule tasks to run automatically — daily, weekly, hourly, or any cadence. Your AI employee works while you sleep.
Morning briefs at 7 AM. Weekly reports on Mondays. Competitor dashboards at 8 AM. Self-improving after every run.
Scheduled tasks Daily / Weekly / Hourly Self-improving
